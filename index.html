Everything you need to know about our wedding coffee service

We serve the entire Tampa Bay area and surrounding communities including Tampa, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Lakeland, Largo, Bradenton, Sarasota, Brandon, Riverview, Wesley Chapel, Town 'n' Country, Plant City, Palm Harbor, Dunedin, Tarpon Springs, Pinellas Park, Seminole, Temple Terrace, Safety Harbor, Gulfport, Oldsmar, New Port Richey, Zephyrhills, Dade City, Brooksville, Spring Hill, Land O' Lakes, Lutz, Valrico, Apollo Beach, Ruskin, Sun City Center, Palmetto, Venice, Ellenton, Parrish, and Siesta Key.

Simply fill out our wedding booking form on our website or call us directly. We'll respond within 24-48 hours with availability and a custom quote tailored to your wedding vision and guest count.

We need a flat surface of 6'x8' and access to a 110-volt outlet. Having worked at numerous wedding venues throughout Florida, we're familiar with most venue requirements and can advise on setup. For outdoor ceremonies, we can provide a generator if needed.

Our wedding coffee service brings the complete café experience directly to your celebration. We provide professional baristas, premium equipment, and artisanal coffee blends. As wedding specialists, we understand how to create special moments and deliver personalized service that makes your day unforgettable.

We require a 50% deposit to confirm your wedding booking. Full refunds are available for cancellations made 90+ days before your wedding, 50% refund for 60-90 days, and no refund within 60 days. We understand that wedding plans can change, so we work with you on rescheduling when possible.
